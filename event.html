Third Thursdays

Join us each month for Third Thursdays. Thornton Park neighborhood merchants, as well as other business and cultural venues throughout Downtown Orlando, participate in this monthly event. Third Thursdays are the perfect opportunity to stroll the neighborhood and check out the many unique shops and restaurants. This is also a great chance to see the ever expanding Downtown Orlando arts and culture scene. Many businesses offer special promotions and discounts on Third Thursdays.

Disco Brunch

On the third Sunday of every month, HUE features its famous Disco Brunch. This is a wonderful chance to relax and enjoy a fun Sunday afternoon. A creative brunch menu offers delicious items appropriately named for the cultural icons of the 80's. A fabulous bloody mary bar and groovy drink specials are popular with the guests. A live DJ provides the perfect backdrop of memorable tunes we all remember and love. Join us the third Sunday of every month for our Disco Brunch. It's a blast! Doors open at 11:00am.

Happy Hour

HUE has great happy hour specials and is the ideal setting to meet with friends, grab a bite on the patio and watch the hustle and bustle of the exciting Thornton Park neighborhood.

HUE Restaurant offers regularly scheduled monthly events. Please call 407.849.1800 for reservations, directions and information.
