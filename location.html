Driving from North Orlando

Interstate 4 west to downtown Orlando, exit at Colonial Drive/Centroplex, turn left on Colonial Drive, heading east. Turn right on Orange Avenue, heading south towards downtown. Turn left on Central Boulevard, heading east. HUE is located a few blocks east on Central, at the intersection of Summerlin and Central.

Driving from South Orlando

Interstate 4 east to downtown Orlando, exit at Amelia Street/Centroplex, turn right on Amelia Street, heading east. Turn right on Orange Avenue, heading south towards downtown. Turn left on Central Boulevard, heading east. HUE is located a few blocks east on Central, at the intersection of Summerlin and Central.

Driving from East Orlando

State Road 408 (East - West Expressway) west to downtown Orlando. Exit at Mills Avenue and travel west on South Street towards downtown Orlando. Turn right heading north on Summerlin Avenue. HUE is located a few blocks north on Summerlin, at the intersection of Summerlin and Central.

Driving from West Orlando

State Road 408 (East - West Expressway) east to downtown Orlando, exit at Orange Avenue (State Road 527) and follow signs north on Orange Avenue to downtown Orlando. Orange Avenue becomes Rosalind Avenue. Turn right on Central Boulevard, heading east. HUE is located a few blocks east on Central, at the intersection of Summerlin and Central.

THORNTON PARK CENTRAL

HUE Restaurant is located in one of downtown's most exciting dining and shopping destinations - Thornton Park Central.

Thornton Park Central is located on the corner of East Central Boulevard and North Summerlin Avenue, just east of downtown Orlando's Central Business District and Lake Eola Park. The restaurant is within walking distance of the Central Business District, downtown hotels and surrounding neighborhoods.

PARKING:

The parking garage for HUE and Thornton Park Central is located behind the restaurant. Parking entrances are located at the west side of the building, on North Eola Drive, and at the north side of the building, on East Washington Street. Nominal fees may apply.
